A Hull Tradition Since 1951

Located near historic Nantasket Beach, the Nantasket Beach Salt Water Club has served generations of Hull residents, beachgoers, boaters, and South Shore families.

The club continues to celebrate the unique maritime traditions and waterfront culture that define the Hull community.

Celebrating 75 Years

From waterfront gatherings to local events, NBSWC continues to honor its members, traditions, and South Shore heritage.

The club remains an important part of the Nantasket Beach and Hull waterfront community.
