A Hull Tradition Since 1951

The Nantasket Beach Salt Water Club has served generations of Hull residents, beachgoers, boaters, and South Shore families for more than seven decades.

Located steps from historic Nantasket Beach, the club represents the spirit of the Massachusetts coastline — community, tradition, and waterfront culture.

Through events, celebrations, charitable outreach, and local traditions, NBSWC continues to be a gathering place for the Hull community.

Celebrating 75 Years

From 1951 to 2026, the Nantasket Beach Salt Water Club continues to honor its members, traditions, and local heritage.

The club remains an important part of the Nantasket waterfront and the greater South Shore community.
